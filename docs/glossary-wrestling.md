# Wrestling Domain Glossary (UWW Aligned)

This glossary defines domain-specific terms strictly aligned with the **United World Wrestling (UWW) International Wrestling Rules**.
It is intended as a **Ubiquitous Language** for software systems modeling UWW wrestling competitions.

Each entry contains:

* **English term** (official or rule-derived)
* **Description (EN)** based on UWW rules
* **English synonyms / related terms** (where applicable)
* **German term(s)** and common synonyms

---

## Bout

**Description (EN):**
A wrestling contest between two wrestlers conducted under UWW rules, consisting of two periods and governed by officials.

**English Synonyms:**
Match

**German Term(s):**
Kampf

**German Synonyms:**
Match, Wettkampf

---

## Wrestler

**Description (EN):**
An athlete licensed by UWW who competes in a bout within a defined age and weight category.

**English Synonyms:**
Competitor, Athlete

**German Term(s):**
Ringer

**German Synonyms:**
Athlet, Wettkämpfer

---

## Weight Category

**Description (EN):**
An official UWW-defined bodyweight division in which wrestlers are eligible to compete.

**English Synonyms:**
Weight Class

**German Term(s):**
Gewichtsklasse

**German Synonyms:**
Gewichtskategorie

---

## Age Category

**Description (EN):**
A UWW-defined age group (e.g. U15, U17, U20, U23, Seniors, Veterans) determining eligibility and bout duration.

**English Synonyms:**
Age Group

**German Term(s):**
Altersklasse

**German Synonyms:**
Alterskategorie

---

## Period

**Description (EN):**
One timed segment of a bout. A bout consists of two periods with a break in between.

**English Synonyms:**
Round

**German Term(s):**
Periode

**German Synonyms:**
Runde, Abschnitt

---

## Mat

**Description (EN):**
The UWW-approved wrestling surface with defined zones, dimensions, and safety areas.

**English Synonyms:**
Wrestling Mat

**German Term(s):**
Matte

**German Synonyms:**
Ringermatte, Wettkampfmatte

## Central Circle

**Description (EN):**
The one-meter diameter circle at the center of the mat used for the start and restart of the bout.

**English Synonyms:**
Center Circle

**German Term(s):**
Zentraler Kreis

**German Synonyms:**
Mittelkreis

---

## Fall

**Description (EN):**
A victory condition achieved when both shoulders of the defensive wrestler are controlled on the mat.

**English Synonyms:**
Pin

**German Term(s):**
Schultersieg

**German Synonyms:**
Fall, Pin
